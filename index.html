Loyverse POS Q&A

Is Loyverse POS really free? ›

Yes! The core Loyverse POS app is completely free to use with no time limits. There are optional paid add-ons for advanced features like employee management and advanced inventory, but the main POS functionality is always free.

How do I use the Loyverse POS system? ›

Download the Loyverse POS app on your smartphone or tablet, create a free account, set up your items, and you're ready to take your first sale. Setup takes just a few minutes.

Does Loyverse POS work offline? ›

Yes, Loyverse POS continues to work when you lose internet connection. Sales are saved locally and synced to the cloud once connectivity is restored.

What kind of hardware can work with Loyverse POS? ›

Loyverse works with a wide range of hardware including receipt printers, barcode scanners, cash drawers, and card readers. It runs on iOS and Android phones and tablets.

Does Loyverse POS have a loyalty card feature? ›

Yes, Loyverse includes a built-in loyalty program that lets you reward customers for repeat purchases with points and discounts.
